About Me

Professional summary

I am a data scientist with a foundation in geospatial analysis, spatial data workflows, and quantitative analytics, supporting environmental, infrastructure, and development-focused initiatives. I have a proven ability to translate complex analytical findings into actionable insights for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

My MSc thesis at the University of Pretoria applies deep learning and computer vision using Google Street View images to analyse urban walkability and accessibility to green public open spaces in the City of Tshwane.

Through roles at Esri Eastern Africa, Gloceps, and Swift Geospatial, I have delivered web & GIS solutions, automated spatial workflows, and built data pipelines that move from raw imagery to evidence that informs planning, conservation, and infrastructure decisions.
